********** WELCOME TO ANDY'S WEBPAGE **********

20210916

Year 1 Bay East, East Capitol, Sun Origin

Left my office in Six Trees at the usual time. A little later than usual now that I think of it. What held me up was the sudden appearance of this geisha named Snowfall.

Her voice was shaky. Like she was about to break down and sob at any moment. But she never did. Somehow she go the idea that I was the one to solve her problems.

There was nothing new about the job. Standard cut and paste. A bit of leg work, but nothing requiring more than a few days. Of course I took the job.

I was two months behind on my payments, and the Eight-Nine-Three enforcers were camped outside of my apartment. That's why I've been working out of a storage room in Six Trees and sleeping in a rent-a-box in the Green Capsule Men's Complex.
